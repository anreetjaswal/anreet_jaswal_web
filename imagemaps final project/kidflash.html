Kidflash

About Kidflash

Kidflash is the sidekick of Flash. He is really fast, his speed gives him a lot of power over is enemys. Kidflash is the funny and clumsy one in this show, but he is a good warrior and superhero. He has a girlfriend and her name is Artemis. Kidflash and Artemis actually retire later on in the show but then later join again for once only for the last mission. Sadly in that last mission Kidflash dies, the whole team was left in shock after that. So Robin left the Justice Legue because he was so upset about the death of Kidflash.
