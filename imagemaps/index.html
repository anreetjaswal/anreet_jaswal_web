Summary of Young Justice

This show is about the sidekicks of the Justice Legue super heros. It all starts on the the foruth of July where the sidekicks get to go to the Hall of Justice for the first time. They all meet there for the first time and get their access number to the hall. Starting from that day they all have been assigned missions as a group and they get the job done. The squad first starts off with only four memebers, which are Kidflash, Aqualad and Robin. Later on in the show three more characters join and their names are Superboy, Missmartian and Artemis. Most of the memebers from this squad have special powers and the rest have talent. This show is also built on love, they all have boyfriends and girlfriends, except Aqualad. Msmartian and Superboy are dating and Artemis and kidflash are dating aswell. All these young superheros have mentors from the Justice Legue. Superboy is the sidekick of Superman, Missmartian is the sidekick and niece of Martian Manhunter, Artemis is the sidekick of Green Arrow, Robin is the sidekick of Batman, Kidflash is the sidekick if Flash and last but not least is Aqualad and he is the sidekick of Aquaman.
